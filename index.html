Why choose CWI?

Our solution is based on the Grupo Awainnova NGC technology for LOW VOLTAGE IONIC ELECTRO-FLOCCULATION TECHNOLOGY WITH D.C. AND D.S.A. ELECTRODES.

ANY TYPE OF WASTE WATER

Regardless of the origin (Industrial, Commercial or Residential), degree of saturation, pH, temperature or type of contamination (organic or inorganic), except radioactive.

ANY LEVEL OF REUSE

We can take the treatment to any degree of cleanliness and fully complying with any ecological standard or any special characterization according to the use required by the end user, for any effluent.

THE BEST COST-BENEFIT RATIO

Even when capital is restricted or without direct investment, operating options can be simple service contract thus eliminating risk. It has never been so easy to produce clean water!

CONFIGURATION FLEXIBILITY THAT ADAPTS TO ANY AVAILABLE SPACE

Our small footprint and extremely flexible plant configuration can adapt to many varied spaces for installation, from parking spaces, basements, service areas and even vertically.



NO ODORS OR RELEASE OF GREENHOUSE GASES

The system is sealed and with flow through treatment there is no fermentation time which prevents the generation of methane which is characteristic of biological based treatment plants.


EFFICIENT, EFFECTIVE AND LOW OPERATING COST PLANTS

We do not depend on additives to operate or the use of chemical products and are not affected by temperature, saturation, PH, nor do we need stabilization time, so the result of the treatment is constant and low-cost maintenance compared to alternative solutions.
